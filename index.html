Why now

Your systems changed. Your software didn't.

Your quote system has run the business for many years on a dated system. It still gets deals out the door. But it can't see across the business. Who owns the follow-up? What's shipping this week? Are commissions tracking against the manufacturer's goal? Did the promo work? How's the new product launch landing? The answers exist, just not in one place. The list goes on.

Your quote system is aging out.

Built for a different era. Slow, dated, no new features coming. Your team works around it, not through it.

Your agency runs across five tools.

Quotes in one tool. CRM in another. Reports built in Excel. Follow-ups buried in the inbox. Meetings happen with numbers read off three different screens. Nothing reconciles without manual work.

Manufacturer scorecards are a guessing game.

Quarterly budgets, manufacturer goals, project pipelines. Are you on pace, behind, by how much? Which open projects can you push to pull in this quarter? Without a real dashboard, you only see the gap at quarter-end. Too late to course-correct.

Padma closes the gap.

Data ingestion

Built for the document tangle your reps actually live in.

Every file type. PDFs, spreadsheets, Word docs, emails. Coming in from distributors, contractors, engineers, end users, and manufacturers.

Padma takes it all in and makes sense of it.

  • Smart uploads. Drop any file and Padma extracts the data.
  • Email ingestion. Emails arrive in any format and Padma takes them in.
  • Manufacturer-aware parsing. Trained on your data, across your agency, your manufacturers, your distributors, and your engineering firms. The moat.
  • Line card aware. Your line card and your manufacturers' terms live in Padma's knowledge base. Specs and RFQs get flagged against the right lines automatically.
  • Direct API connections. For manufacturers that have them, we wire them up. Live pricing, order status, inventory, order entry, and more.
  • Covers everything. Price lists, SPAs, stock programs, statements, project specs, and much more. All searchable in seconds in Padma's knowledge base.
